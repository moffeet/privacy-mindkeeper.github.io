数据删除说明

MindKeeper 尊重您的隐私权和数据控制权。您可以随时要求删除您的账号和相关数据。

删除步骤

  1. 在应用内：
    • 打开 MindKeeper 应用
    • 进入"设置"页面
    • 点击"账号管理"
    • 选择"删除账号和数据"
  2. 通过电子邮件：
    • 发送邮件至：jibaichang@gmail.com
    • 邮件主题：请求删除账号
    • 在邮件中提供您的Google账号邮箱地址

数据删除范围

当您请求删除账号时，我们将：

  • 立即删除：
    • 您的账号信息
    • 云端存储的所有应用使用数据
    • 与您账号关联的所有设置和偏好
  • 本地数据：
    • 应用卸载时自动删除
    • 您也可以在应用设置中手动清除
  • 数据保留：
    • 出于法律合规要求，某些交易记录可能会保留最长90天
    • 匿名的使用统计数据可能会被保留用于改进服务

处理时间

我们将在收到您的请求后的30天内完成数据删除。删除完成后，我们会通过电子邮件通知您。

注意事项

  • 删除操作不可撤销
  • 删除后，您的所有数据将无法恢复
  • 如果您使用了应用内购买功能，请在删除账号前确认相关事项

Data Deletion Instructions

MindKeeper respects your privacy rights and data control. You can request to delete your account and associated data at any time.

Deletion Steps

  1. In the app:
    • Open MindKeeper app
    • Go to "Settings"
    • Tap "Account Management"
    • Select "Delete Account and Data"
  2. Via email:
    • Send an email to: jibaichang@gmail.com
    • Subject: Account Deletion Request
    • Include your Google account email address in the message

Data Deletion Scope

When you request account deletion, we will:

  • Immediate Deletion:
    • Your account information
    • All cloud-stored app usage data
    • All settings and preferences associated with your account
  • Local Data:
    • Automatically deleted when the app is uninstalled
    • Can be manually cleared in app settings
  • Data Retention:
    • Some transaction records may be retained for up to 90 days for legal compliance
    • Anonymous usage statistics may be retained for service improvement

Processing Time

We will complete the data deletion within 30 days of receiving your request. You will be notified via email when the deletion is complete.

Important Notes

  • Deletion operations are irreversible
  • All data will be unrecoverable after deletion
  • If you have made in-app purchases, please review related matters before account deletion
